Alternative Delivery Types

Face-to-Face

Traditional on-campus courses. Classes meet at a scheduled time and place.

Online Synchronous

100% online and a student never has to come to campus, but the online portion is at scheduled times. Those scheduled times are noted in the meeting time patterns for students to know.

Online Asynchronous

100% online and a student never has to come to campus or meet at any scheduled times. Scheduled times should NOT be noted in the meeting time patterns.

Hybrid Synchronous

Some synchronous online and some face to face where a student must come to campus. Scheduled times for both synchronous online and face to face should be noted in the meeting time patterns for the students to know.

Hybrid Asynchronous

Some asynchronous online and some face to face where a student must come to campus. Scheduled times for the face to face should be noted in the meeting time patterns for the students to know.

Interactive Video Distance Learning

Course section requires a student to attend class at a location with Interactive Video Distance Learning equipment

Study Away

Course section is offered off campus travel (inside the United States and US territories.) Additional program fees may apply and an application for acceptance must be obtained from www.studyabroad.miamioh.edu
